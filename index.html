Welcome to EZ-sweets

At EZ-sweets, we love making delicious treats that bring smiles to our customers' faces. Our small team of dedicated bakers works hard every day to create fresh, tasty desserts using simple, quality ingredients and lots of care.

From our homestyle cakes to warm cinnamon buns, everything is baked fresh daily. Whether you're picking up a quick morning treat or need something sweet for a special occasion, we'd love to help make your day a little sweeter.

Our Menu

Macarons

Our colorful macarons come in a variety of flavors like chocolate, vanilla, raspberry, and seasonal specials. Each one is carefully made to be crispy on the outside and soft on the inside - perfect with your afternoon coffee!

Chocolate Layer Cake

A classic favorite! Our chocolate layer cake features moist chocolate cake layers with rich chocolate frosting. Great for birthdays or any time you need a chocolate fix.

Cheesecake

Our creamy New York style cheesecake sits on a graham cracker crust and is baked to perfection. Try it plain or with fresh fruit topping - you can't go wrong!

Muffins

Start your day with our freshly baked muffins. Choose from blueberry, chocolate chip, banana nut, and more. They're always fresh out of the oven each morning!

Cinnamon Buns

Soft, warm, and loaded with cinnamon-sugar goodness. Each bun is topped with our creamy frosting. Perfect for breakfast or an afternoon treat!
